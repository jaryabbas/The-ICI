Islamic Festivals & Commemorations

Muharram – The Month of Mourning

1 Muharram – Islamic New Year

Marks the beginning of the new Islamic year.

2 Muharram – Arrival of Imam Hussain (a) in Karbala

Imam Hussain (a) and his companions arrive in Karbala.

7 Muharram – Water Supply to Imam Hussain's Camp is Blocked

The army of Yazid denies Imam Hussain (a) and his family access to water, increasing their suffering.

10 Muharram (Ashura) – Martyrdom of Imam Hussain (a)

The tragic day when Imam Hussain (a) and his family were martyred.

25 Muharram – Martyrdom of Imam Zainul Abidin (a)

Poisoned and martyred in Medina.

Safar – The Month of Sorrow

20 Safar (Arbaeen) – 40th Day of Imam Hussain’s Martyrdom

Millions visit Karbala in devotion.

28 Safar – Demise of Holy Prophet Muhammad (s) & Martyrdom of Imam Hasan (a)

The greatest loss in Islam.

Rabi' al-Awwal – The Month of Light

12-17 Rabi' al-Awwal – Birth of Prophet Muhammad (s) & Imam Jafar Sadiq (a)

Two luminous personalities in Islamic history is born in one of these blessed day.

Jumada al-Awwal

13 Jumada al-Awwal – Martyrdom of Lady Fatima (a)

One of the possible dates of Lady Fatima's (a) martyrdom.

Rajab – The Month of Spirituality

1 Rajab – Birth of Imam Muhammad al-Baqir (a)

A beacon of knowledge and wisdom.

13 Rajab – Birth of Imam Ali (a)

The only person born inside the Kaaba.

Sha’ban – The Month of Celebration

3 Sha’ban – Birth of Imam Hussain (a)

The hero of Karbala.

4 Sha’ban – Birth of Hazrat Abbas (a)

The flagbearer of Karbala.

5 Sha’ban – Birth of Imam Zainul Abidin (a)

Known for his supplications and devotion.

15 Sha’ban – Birth of Imam Mahdi (a)

The awaited savior.

Ramadan – The Month of Revelation

10 Ramadan – Demise of Lady Khadija (a)

The beloved wife of Prophet Muhammad (s).

19 Ramadan – Attack on Imam Ali (a)

Struck while in prayer.

21 Ramadan – Martyrdom of Imam Ali (a)

The loss of the first Imam.

23 Ramadan – Laylatul Qadr

The Night of Power when the Quran was revealed.

Shawwal

1 Shawwal – Eid al-Fitr

Festival marking the end of Ramadan.

25 Shawwal – Martyrdom of Imam Jafar Sadiq (a)

The sixth Imam and founder of the Jafari school of thought.

Dhul Qa’dah

11 Dhul Qa’dah – Birth of Imam Ali al-Ridha (a)

The eighth Imam of Islam.

Dhul Hijjah – The Month of Pilgrimage

1 Dhul Hijjah – Marriage Anniversary of Lady Fatima (a) and Imam Ali (a)

A blessed union.

8-12 Dhul Hijjah – Hajj (Pilgrimage)

The annual pilgrimage to Makkah.

10 Dhul Hijjah – Eid al-Adha

The Festival of Sacrifice.

13 Dhul Hijjah – Martyrdom of Muslim ibn Aqil (a)

The loyal envoy of Imam Hussain (a) in Kufa.

18 Dhul Hijjah – Eid al-Ghadeer

Prophet Muhammad (s) declared Imam Ali (a) as his successor.

24 Dhul Hijjah – Event of Mubahala

The event where the Prophet (s) proved the truth of Islam.
